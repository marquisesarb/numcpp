### Lewis

- [1] Option valuation under stochastic volatility - Lewis (2000) : https://econpapers.repec.org/bookchap/vsvvbooks/ovsv.htm

Where, given a well defined characteristic function for a specific stochastic process, one can obtain a semi closed form of the european option price. One must note that the "normalized" european option price is implemented here folowing the formulation from P. Jäckel (Let's be rational).
