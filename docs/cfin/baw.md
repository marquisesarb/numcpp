### Barone Adesi Whaley 

Implementation of the analytic "closed form" formula for pricing an american vanilla option. See the scientific paper for details: 
- [1] Efficient analytic approximation of American Option Values - Barone-Adesi and Whaley (1987) : https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1540-6261.1987.tb02569.x

With inspiration to the work of Jäckel, an efficient algortihm is implemented to get the implied volatility from an american option price written on a Future (where no drift is necessary).
